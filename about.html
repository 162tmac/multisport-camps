About The Camps

Basic Info

  • Our camps run for one week. Dates will be posted soon.
  • The camp lasts for 4 hours a day. Starting at 10am and ending at 2pm.
  • Pricing:
    • €70 for 1 child
    • €130 for 2
    • €180 for 3

Goals

In the camps, we try to engage and introduce kids to various different sports and games.

Our goal is to get more and active and have fun!

Activities

In the camps, kids will get the chance to play many different sports. Activities include:

  • Soccer
  • Gaelic Football
  • Hurling
  • Rounders
  • Frisbee
  • Athletics

What to bring

  • Packed Lunch
  • Football boots, or else runners.
  • Gear for training (jersey, shorts, etc.)
  • Lots of water and drinks
