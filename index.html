MultiSport Summer Camps

Created and run by PE teachers. Each camp is a week long.

Our goal is to get kids active and have some fun!

More Info
Get Active & Have Fun

Opportunity to play many different sports and activities throughout the camps.

Sports and activities include Soccer, Gaelic Football, Hurling, Rounders, Ultimate Frisbee, Athletics, Olympic Handball, Tag Rugby and many more

Run by PE Teachers

The camps have been running for three years and were started by a group of PE teachers, who share 25 years of experience teaching GAA, soccer, rugby and other sports.

We understand how important fitness and being active is and we try to promote a healthy lifestyle through sport.
