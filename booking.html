Online Booking

Booking details

Click the button below to proceed to Easy Payments Plus web page to complete booking form and payment
for your child or children to participate in Summer Multisport Camps:

Below are a few helpful tips to complete the booking:

1. Register an account on Easy Payments Plus as a parent.

2. Please read Terms and Conditions on Home Screen before filling out form.

3. To view information booklet about the particular camp, click on the paragraph for that camp underneath “Description” for the information leaflet for each venue’s camp.

The following steps are to book your child:

4. Click “Add to Bill” button for each child and fill in their details.

5. For any mobile number section on the forms, please use parent’s mobile number only.

6. Please check your bill at the top of page to ensure it is correct before clicking ‘Proceed to Payment’.

List of prices to compare your bill to are as follows:

  • 1 child = €60
  • 2 children = €110
  • 3 children = €150
  • 4 children = €190

7. If any problems arise, please call, WhatsApp or text Tom on 0876884136.
